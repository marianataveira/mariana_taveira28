UPACK

In this work based on the theme “protect the enviroment” a fictional company was created.The U.pack brand aims to intervene in the food sector of the market, reducing the use of plastic, a material that increasingly exists in large quantities that harm, contaminate and kill ecosystems. To present a possible solution to this problem, U.Pack created packaging for various types of food sold in bulk, where the customer can place the desired amount without resorting to the plastic bag, that is an ecological and sustainable and reusable alternative. These packages, in addition to being spreadable and easy to assemble, can also be reused by consumers during several trips to the supermarket.
