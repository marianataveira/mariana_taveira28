BOWLING

The design of the Bowling font is a typographic design project, arising with the proposal of creating a typographic font. This font is recognizable for its rounded shapes, contrast and little counter space. It is a typographic family with a linear serif and spur. The Bowling font design is an original typographic design, with the aim of creating a typographic font recognizable by its rounded shapes, contrast and little counter space. For this, it was necessary to study the typographic development processes and the relationship between the letters.
