ASTRID

The work was the modification of a table based on the proposal to deconstruct an existing one, adapting the form and content to an alternative technology, sheet metal, assuming the need to attribute the necessary characteristics of adaptation to a habitable space to the presented solution. These changes consist mainly of overcoming some constraints related to the new technology but also in terms of usability. There was a need to reuse the metal plate, using only three pieces: one that forms the drawer, one that forms the feet, and the other one that is the respective top. The colour palette was adopted to allow inserting this bedside table in environments that follow the Nordic / Scandinavian style, bringing clarity and a sense of serenity: White, Black and Yellow.
