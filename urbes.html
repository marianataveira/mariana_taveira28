Urbes

Inspired by the Lionesa mural, one of the biggest art murals in Portugal, and traditional commerce, this piece consists of the deconstruction and representation of some key elements for the development of society and the economy (urban art and traditional commerce) that, with the daily routine and agitation of the modern, tends to be ignored and falling into oblivion. This textile sculpture was created to alert the viewer to pay attention to what surrounds him and to, despite all this unrest, value what, today, tends to be forgotten.
