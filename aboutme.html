ABOUT ME

Born in Oporto on July 28th, 2000. Until the age of 18 she lives with her two families, her father and her mother, and her brother, 5 years younger. She studied at Colégio Luso-Francês, a catholic college, since she was 3 years old, which helped her being a girl with marked values, above all, by respect for others and responsibility in her actions. At the age of 15 she joined the Soares dos Reis Artistic school, with a grade 5/5. With a natural gift to create, her pieces are the result of a still short journey, where the persistence of following her dream of achieving something that completes her as an artist and designer is evident. In 2018 applied to the Design Course at the University of Aveiro with a grade of 171/ 200 , an institution where she started her studies as a Designer, a dream that she follows since child. Since then her passion in Design grows visibly, always trying to explore new paths and seeking to deepen his knowledge. As hobbies she develops activities in areas as volunteer with national and international experiences, babysitting, photography, makeup, theatre and cooking, areas in which some of them held courses and workshops. This portfolio reflects the beginning of what is expected to be a long stage in professional and personal growth.
